Your internet roadmap to digital resources to help you study, become, and work as a technical writer

Introduction

Technical writing is the communicative force behind industry. Without it, communication comes to a standstill and progress ceases. Your decison to become a techinical writer means you will plan an intricate role in shaping the industry in which you find yourself. This website will serve as your personal guide to graduate schools, internships, professional associations, and publications regarding your choosen career.

Professional Organizations

Click on each image to find out more

IEEE Professional Communication Society

This society seems to be focused on bridging the gap between engineering and writing. They hope to advance the importance and scope of technical writing and writers in the engineering community. They also exist to help mitigate communication problems between writers, editors scientists, and engineers.

Center for Plain Language

Their motto is “make it clear,” and they exist so that more language might be just that. They educate professionals about plain language, as well as critiquing their use of it. They yearly audit the use of plain language in government agencies. This can be helpful to you because they provide refreshers on plain language, as well as being a guide to how well your company or agency uses it.

Society for Technical Communications

This group serves its members by advancing the scope of technical communication in all fields. They work to promote the importance of changing complex language into easy to understand, plain language. They also help their members find the most beneficial practices and opportunities within the field of technical communication.

Association of Teachers of Technical Writing

This group promotes the discipline of teaching technical writing/communication. They work to make people and organizations more aware of the importance of technical communication teachers. They provide data, articles, and other information that might be helpful to a teacher in the field. They also like to recognize and publish distinguished works from their members.

Professional Publications

Click on each image to find out more

Technical Communication Quarterly

This publication, from the Association of Teachers of Technical Writing provides readers with valuable research and data regarding the entire spectrum of technical writing. Each edition goes into great detail regarding the rhetoric, methodology, and importance of the subject matter.

Journal of Technical Writing and Communication

This quarterly publication strives to provide useful documents to workers in all field of the profession. From teaching to industry team management, the people who publish this hope to reach all technical writers and provide a repository of helpful documents to them.

Intercom

From the people that brought you the Society for Technical Communication, comes the publication known as Intercom. They focus on providing articles that detail tips and tricks of the trade, specifically for industry writers. This publication also hosts yearly awards for things like excellent magazines, journals and electronic creations.
